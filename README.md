# Sakhi – Women’s Health Awareness App

Sakhi is a women’s health awareness Android application designed to help users identify early symptoms of common but often ignored health conditions such as anemia, PCOS, and thyroid disorders. The app focuses on awareness, engagement, and timely healthcare support through interactive features and personalized guidance.

---

## Objective
To empower women with health awareness by providing symptom tracking, surveys, reminders, and nearby healthcare access through a simple and user-friendly mobile application.

---

## Key Features
- User authentication and profile management
- Symptom tracking and health status monitoring
- AI-based health chatbot for guidance and awareness
- Health surveys for understanding user conditions
- Reminder functionality for health-related activities
- Daily challenges and wellness-based activities
- Myth vs Fact health awareness module
- Health articles for common women’s health issues
- Nearby care feature to locate healthcare services
- Feedback collection from users
- Clean and intuitive user interface

---

## Application Modules / Tabs
- Home – Daily health status, challenges, and articles
- Period – Period-related tracking and information
- Survey – Health-related surveys and assessments
- Nearby Care – Location-based healthcare assistance
- Chat – AI-powered health chatbot

---

## Tech Stack
- Android (Java/Kotlin)
- XML for UI Design
- Supabase (Authentication and Database)
- Android Studio

---

## App Architecture
The application follows a modular Android architecture:
- **UI Layer** – Activities, fragments, and XML layouts
- **Logic Layer** – Symptom analysis, chatbot logic, reminders, and surveys
- **Data Layer** – Supabase for authentication and data storage

This architecture ensures better scalability, maintainability, and separation of concerns.
